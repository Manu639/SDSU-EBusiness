It's old, but the best, mouth-watering recipes are made in that kitchen.

The restaurant Sobrino de Botin opened its doors in 1725 and has been awarded by different organizations since then. Forbes Magazine gave the third place in their World's Top 10 classic restaurants.
The Guinness Book of Records has gave them officialy the oldest restaurant title and it has become a benchmark for the traditional cuisine.
